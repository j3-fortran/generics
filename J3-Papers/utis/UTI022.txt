Introduction:
=============

UTI022 is concerned with an inconsistency regarding procedure
pointers.  The original text in paper 25-204r3 has a constraint
disallowing a specific procedure that is a procedure pointer as an
instantiation argument.  But there was no analogous constraint on
procedure pointers accessed via a generic name.

Subgroup agrees that this inconsistency should be fixed.  We intended
the restriction to avoid the complexity of whether the resulting
instantiation then changes behavior when said procedure pointer is
reassigned during execution or does the behavior remain rooted on the
original association.  There may well be interesting use cases that
could benefit from such run-time variation, but we prefer a
conservative approach for this first iteration.
